Our Story

Since 1980

A business established by Mr.Arnold and Mrs.Anderson. AAA Bistro was founded on July 14,1980, starting from a small cafe that serve steak and beer on Jalan Raya Darmo, Surabaya.

The Beginning of AAA Bistro

Before opening AAA Bistro in Surabaya, Mr.Arnold and Mrs.Anderson had a livestock business by breeding cows, goat, chicken, ostrich, etc. The business evantually became the forerunner to the birth of AAA Bistro.
